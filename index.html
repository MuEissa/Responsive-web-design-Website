User Segmentation Analysis

The analysis of user behavior on a food delivery platform reveals that revenue is primarily driven by Finland, Denmark, and Greece, with restaurants being the most popular store type. Preferred cuisines include American, Italian, Japanese, Mexican, and Middle Eastern, while iOS users spend more than Android users. Most customers are infrequent low-spenders, averaging 20 days between orders and spending under €100 per transaction—key insights for optimizing marketing, UX, and expansion strategies.

  • Date: February 2023
  • Client: User Segmentation Analysis
  • Category: Analytics
